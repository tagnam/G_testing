About Us

GOODAID is a team of exceptionally talented people, who are not satisfied with the status quo of the current pharmaceutical ecosystem of our country.

We are bringing access to a portfolio of high quality pharmaceuticals, across prescribed (Rx) and non prescribed(Non -Rx/OTC) formulations, establishing new benchmarks of Quality and Accessibility.

Quality today means being authentic, being functional, being sustainable and being sensibly priced. We are committed to these principles without T&Câ€™s

We are here to carry forward the work of scientists and innovators who discovered these life saving drugs. All our products leverage generic formulations (Drugs that were once patented and are now available for the world to access and operate in a fair market), manufactured and tested as per the rigorous standards laid down by World Health Organisation & FDA.

Our Mission

To deliver Accessible Healthcare via Highest Quality Generics and sharing Actionable Tools, Inspirational Stories, Decoded Knowledge with everyone.

Power to you to be Healthy & Happy and Pay it forward.
